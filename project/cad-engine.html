V6 Engine Assembly

Category: CAD Design

Tools: SolidWorks

Project Description

This project involved the complete CAD design and assembly of a V6 internal combustion engine. The goal was to model realistic components and validate mechanical motion.

Engineering Approach

Parametric modeling techniques were used for all components. Assembly constraints and mates were applied to simulate real motion. Interference checks and motion studies were performed.

Design Visuals

Results & Learnings

The final assembly demonstrated smooth operation without interferences. This project strengthened understanding of complex assemblies and real-world mechanical constraints.
