Medical Chair with Integrated Heating System

CAD design and functional modification of a medical chair incorporating localized heating elements to improve patient comfort during extended use.

SolidWorks CAD Design Ergonomics Medical Device

Project Overview

This project involves the CAD modeling and modification of a medical chair designed for clinical environments. The main objective was to integrate heating elements within the chair structure while maintaining safety, ergonomics, and structural integrity.

Key Design Features

  • Ergonomic seat, backrest, and leg support for patient comfort
  • Integrated heating zones positioned in critical contact areas
  • Stable wheeled base allowing mobility within medical facilities
  • Storage compartments integrated into the base structure
  • Design optimized for ease of cleaning and maintenance

Tools & Skills Used

SolidWorks Parametric Modeling Assembly Design Product Modification Ergonomic Design

Engineering Considerations

Special care was taken to ensure that the heating elements did not interfere with mechanical components or patient safety. The design balances comfort, manufacturability, and reliability, making it suitable for real-world medical applications.

Outcome

The final CAD model demonstrates a practical and well-integrated medical chair design that enhances patient comfort through heating while preserving structural robustness and clinical usability.
