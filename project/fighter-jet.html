Fighter Jet Surface CAD Model

High-detail surface CAD model of a fighter aircraft focusing on aerodynamic form, smooth curvature continuity, and realistic aircraft geometry.

Surface Modeling SolidWorks Aerospace CAD Complex Geometry

Model Visualization Video

Project Overview

This project involves the surface modeling of a modern fighter jet using advanced CAD techniques. The model emphasizes aerodynamic shaping, blended wing–body transitions, and smooth surface continuity across the fuselage, wings, and control surfaces.

Design Approach

The aircraft was modeled using surface features such as boundary surfaces, lofts, and guide curves to achieve precise control over curvature and form. Particular attention was given to leading edges, canopy integration, air intakes, and external payload mounting points to closely resemble real-world fighter aircraft design principles.

Key Learnings

This project significantly improved my understanding of surface-based CAD modeling, aerodynamic form development, and complex geometry management. It strengthened my ability to create smooth, manufacturable surfaces suitable for aerospace and high-performance engineering applications.
