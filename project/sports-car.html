Project Description

This project involves the complete surface modeling of a high-performance sports car. Emphasis was placed on smooth Class-A surfaces, aerodynamic efficiency, and realistic proportions. The model includes detailed exterior components such as headlights, hood vents, rear wing, and wheel arches.

The objective was to develop advanced surfacing skills in SolidWorks while maintaining manufacturable geometry and visually appealing design suitable for automotive applications.
