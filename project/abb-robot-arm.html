ABB Robot Arm

Industrial robotic arm CAD model designed to replicate real-world articulated robot geometry used in automated manufacturing systems.

CAD Design SolidWorks Mechanical Assembly Robotics

Project Overview

This project focuses on the complete CAD modeling of an ABB-style industrial robotic arm. Each component was modeled as an individual part and assembled using appropriate mates to achieve realistic rotational motion across all joints.

Design Approach

The arm consists of a rigid base, shoulder, elbow, and wrist mechanisms designed to simulate real industrial robot kinematics. Emphasis was placed on correct proportions, clean geometry, and assembly constraints to ensure mechanical realism.

Motion Demonstration

Key Learnings

This project strengthened my understanding of complex CAD assemblies, joint constraints, and robotic arm structure. It also improved my ability to model industrial-grade mechanical systems suitable for automation and manufacturing applications.
